01
Upload Your Bowling Video

Film from any angle using your phone — side-on, behind the bowler, or broadcast footage. BowlFast.AI handles variable frame rates, motion blur, and multiple people in frame automatically. No special equipment needed.

Any Camera · Any Angle · Any Format
02
AI Maps Your Action

Our computer vision pipeline isolates the bowler from other players, maps 14+ skeletal landmarks per frame using MediaPipe pose estimation, and tracks each phase of the delivery — from run-up through to follow-through.

MediaPipe Pose · YOLOv8 · Real-Time Processing
03
Biomechanical Report Generated

Every key metric is calculated — spine tilt, hip-shoulder separation, arm extension angle, stride consistency, follow-through alignment, and more. Each is rated and contextualised against safe performance ranges.

14+ Metrics · Injury Risk Flags · Rated Output
04
Coaching Feedback & Drills

Based on your metrics, BowlFast.AI generates plain-language coaching feedback and a drill programme targeted at your specific weaknesses — the same kind of actionable guidance a professional bowling coach would give.

Personalised Drills · Plain Language Feedback

Analysis Engine

Real Data.
Real Coaching.

BowlFast.AI doesn't guess. Every metric comes from computer vision analysis of your actual video — not estimates or generalised averages. What you see is what your body is actually doing.

Injury Prevention First

Fast bowlers miss more cricket than any other player type. BowlFast.AI flags the mechanical patterns that lead to lumbar stress fractures, side strains, and knee injuries — before they happen.

Frame-Level Precision

Analysis happens at the individual frame level — not averaged across the delivery. You see exactly what your spine, hips, and shoulders are doing at BFC, FFC, and release.

Plain Language Output

No jargon. Every metric comes with a plain-language explanation, a risk rating, and a specific drill to address it — ready to take straight into training.
