Key Features

Built for Every Fast Bowler

🎯 Injury Prevention First

Fast bowlers miss more cricket than any other player type. BowlFast.AI flags the mechanical patterns that lead to lumbar stress fractures, side strains, and knee injuries — before they happen.

Analysis happens at the individual frame level — not averaged across the delivery. You see exactly what your spine, hips, and shoulders are doing at BFC, FFC, and release.

No jargon. Every metric comes with a plain-language explanation, a risk rating, and a specific drill to address it — ready to take straight into training.

Train like an international player, with or without a coaching team. Just your phone, your action, and the data.
